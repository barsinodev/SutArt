FAQ

  • The PDF is not generating correctly.
    Make sure you are using the latest version of the app and that your device has enough free storage. If the issue persists, send us a short description of the project (frame size and photo type) and, if possible, screenshots of the error.
  • Colors in the mosaic don't match my LEGO® collection.
    StudArt uses a limited palette inspired by official LEGO® colors. Depending on lighting and screen calibration, colors may look slightly different. You can adapt the closest bricks you own when building.
  • Ads feel too frequent.
    StudArt may use banner or interstitial ads to support development. Ads should never block core interactions for long. If you see an ad that misbehaves or overlaps the UI, please send us a screenshot and your device model.
  • Data & Privacy.
    You can review how we handle ads and privacy in our Privacy Policy and User Privacy Choices.
