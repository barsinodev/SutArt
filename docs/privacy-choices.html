User Privacy Choices — StudArt

Last updated: 2025-12-01

In the app

  1. Show the consent form again (Google UMP): in the app’s Settings menu, open the privacy section to show the Google consent form again and update your ad preferences (personalised vs non-personalised ads).
  2. Turn off personalised ads: if you do not want personalised ads, select that option when the consent form appears or from the setting above. You will still see ads, but they will not be based on your past activity.

On iOS (system level)

  1. App Tracking Transparency (ATT): go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking and either disable “Allow Apps to Request to Track” or disable tracking specifically for StudArt.
  2. Apple Advertising (where available): review the options under Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising.

Privacy choices — quick summaries

English

You can always reopen the Google UMP consent form inside StudArt to switch between personalised and non-personalised ads. On iOS, manage tracking permissions for StudArt under Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.
