How it works

StudArt is designed so you can go from photo to a physical brick frame in minutes. You only need your iPhone and your bricks.

1

Choose the frame size

Select the baseplate size (for example 16×16) and how many you want horizontally and vertically.

2

Upload your favourite photo

Portraits, pets, landscapes… Adjust the crop and let StudArt convert it into a 1×1 stud mosaic.

3

Generate PDF instructions

Get a parts summary, the final mosaic and a printable PDF with all the steps to build your frame.
